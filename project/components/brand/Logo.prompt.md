Makler-Content wordmark with the "M" mark — a token-built **placeholder** until the final logo lands.

```jsx
<Logo size="md" />
<Logo size="lg" onDark />        {/* on sage / photo grounds */}
<Logo markOnly />                {/* favicon / avatar */}
```

`onDark` swaps the mark to terracotta and the wordmark to linen. `markOnly` renders just the sage "M" tile.
